Mickey Mouse.

Mickey Mouse, created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks, debuted in "Steamboat Willie" in 1928 and quickly became an iconic figure in animation. Known for his cheerful personality and distinctive red shorts, yellow shoes, and white gloves, Mickey embodies the spirit of joy and adventure. As the mascot of The Walt Disney Company, he continues to delight audiences worldwide with his timeless charm.
